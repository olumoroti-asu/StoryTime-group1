I had finally arrived in Berlin. All of a sudden, a Hund wearing lederhose sprinted through the biergarten. 
The hund grabbed a bratwurst and jumped into a porsche speeding away down the hauptstrasse. 
The driver turned up the klimaanlage and made his way to the flughafen, so the hund could catch his flight home to his schloss.
The hund liked to eat his favorite bratwurst at the flughafen while listening to Rammstein.
